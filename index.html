About Laura

Born deep in the Spanish mountains, Laura always knew she wanted to be an artist. After spending her childhood creating the most amazing drawings, she decided to take her skills to coding.

After some obviously irrelevant jobs (can you believe she worked for a bank??) she ended up at FUGA. All was going well, until that one faithfull day: October 24th, 2018

The day started as any other, but then came that one innocent seeming sentence, spoken by her colleague Dima: "Why don't you pick up that case for the cloning service". That one sentence would change her life.

After spending a month in FUGA's deepest darkest corners, she realized she had had enough. She needed something grand, something to forget everything she had seen that past month. And so a course in fashion design was an easy choice.

Now, close to a year later, it is clear that this was the right choice. She spends her days making the people of FUGA smile, and her nights making sure the people at FUGA look amazing. As one of her colleagues famously said, Coder by day, Master sewer by night!
